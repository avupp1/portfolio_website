SolidWorks Vending Machine Design & Animation

Executive Summary

A fully constrained mechanical vending system modeled to simulate coordinated dispensing mechanisms within a manufacturable assembly architecture. The design integrates kinematic motion validation, tolerance control, and interference analysis to ensure reliable component interaction.

Mission Context

Mechanical dispensing systems require coordinated motion, controlled tolerances, and reliable assembly integration to achieve consistent functionality.

System Architecture

A multi-component assembly was constructed within SolidWorks to simulate dispensing and structural support subsystems. Tolerance considerations guided interface refinement.

Technical Analysis

Motion simulations evaluated dynamic behavior and identified potential constraint conflicts within moving subassemblies. Interference analysis ensured realistic mechanical interaction.

Validation and Performance

Simulation confirmed smooth dispensing behavior and successful integration of moving components within manufacturable limits.

Role and Impact

Team Member

  • Full CAD modeling and assembly construction
  • Motion validation and interference analysis
  • Tolerance refinement
  • Technical documentation
