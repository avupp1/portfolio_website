Lunar Pit and Cave Explorer (L’PACE)

Sep 2024 - Dec 2024

A dual rover lunar mission concept focused on exploring the Marius Hills pit as a potential site for future human habitation. By combining surface and subsurface investigation, the system studies radiation shielding, thermal stability, structural integrity, and the presence of volatiles. The project explores how robotic systems could answer some of the most important questions about living beneath the lunar surface.

On-Site Refueling Device - Universal Magnetic Docking Interface

Jan 2025 - Apr 2025

A next generation spacecraft refueling concept designed to simplify how vehicles connect and transfer propellant in orbit. The system replaces complex mechanical docking hardware with a self aligning magnetic interface to reduce mass, wear, and potential failure points. The design rethinks how sustainable refueling could support long duration lunar and deep space missions.
